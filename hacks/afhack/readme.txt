Auto Fire Hack 1.0                       Till Harbaum
-----------------------------------------------------

Auto Fire Hack will increase your hit rate in some
game dramatically (e.g. zap2000).

To enable auto fire for a specific hard key, go
to the preference dialog of AutoFire Hack in hackmaster,
select the button the game uses for 'fire' (e.g.
the memo button in zap2000) and from now on the button
will be pressed and released automatically whenever you
just hold down that button.
